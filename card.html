Brown Bear



Mating of brown bears takes place from May to July.

  • Scientific Name: Ursus arctos
  • Mass: 180 - 1,300 lbs (Adult)
  • Height: 2.3 - 5 ft.
  • Tropic Level: Omnivorous

The brown bear is the most widely distributed bear and is found across much of northern Eurasia and North America.
